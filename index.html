About Us
Healthcare Staffing Agency

Quanka Healthcare Staffing Agency is a reputable and professional staffing agency that has been in operation for three years. It is operated by Nurses who have over ten years of hospital and Nursing Home experience and very knowledgeable in the healthcare industry. We specialize in providing healthcare professionals to healthcare facilities, organizations, and families. With a strong commitment to excellence, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner in the healthcare industry. Our agency understands the critical importance of having qualified and reliable healthcare professionals to deliver exceptional patient care. We work closely with healthcare facilities, including hospitals, clinics, long-term care facilities, and other healthcare settings, to fulfill their staffing needs efficiently and effectively.

Qunaka Healthcare Agency takes pride in our rigorous screening and selection process. We carefully evaluate and verify the qualifications, experience, credentials, and references of all healthcare professionals in our network. Our team of experienced recruiters and healthcare industry experts ensures that we match the right professionals to the specific requirements and culture of each healthcare facility. We offer a wide range of healthcare staffing solutions, including temporary, contract, and permanent placements. Our extensive network of healthcare professionals encompasses various specialties, such as registered nurses, registered practical nurses, and personal support workers.

As a staffing agency, we are committed to providing exceptional customer service. We strive to develop long-term partnerships with our clients, understanding their unique needs and challenges. Our dedicated account managers work closely with clients to ensure a seamless staffing process, timely response to staffing requests, and ongoing support to address any concerns or issues that may arise. In addition to our commitment to clients, we are equally devoted to the success and satisfaction of our healthcare professionals. We offer competitive compensation packages, flexible work options, professional development opportunities, and a supportive work environment. Our goal is to empower healthcare professionals to thrive in their careers while making a positive impact on patient outcomes.

Quanka Health care Staffing Agency operates with integrity, transparency, and adherence to ethical standards. We prioritize compliance with all relevant healthcare regulations, including licensing, certification, and privacy laws. Confidentiality and data security are paramount in all our operations. We continuously strive for innovation and improvement, leveraging technology and industry best practices to optimize our staffing processes and enhance client and healthcare professional experiences. Our commitment to excellence and our passion for the healthcare industry drive us to continually raise the bar in healthcare staffing. By choosing Quanka Healthcare Staffing Agency, healthcare facilities/organizations gain a trusted partner that understands their staffing challenges and provides top-tier healthcare professionals. Healthcare professionals benefit from rewarding opportunities, career growth, and a supportive agency that values their expertise and contributions.

We are proud of our role in supporting the healthcare community and look forward to serving as your preferred healthcare staffing agency.
